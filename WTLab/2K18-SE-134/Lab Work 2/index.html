Vison

“To be one of the premium departments in the world by focusing on innovative research and quality education”.

Mission

The Mission of the department is as follows:

  1. To inculcate theoretical and practical knowledge of software development and engineering practices according to global industry standards in the students.
  2. To develop innovative mindset among the students through various research initiatives, opportunities and programs.
  3. To develop industry-ready software engineers by imparting knowledge through skill-based and technical trainings in collaboration with software industries.
  4. To instil an attitude for social good by providing opportunities to work with community-sensitive organizations.
  5. To foster personnel, professional, and research ethics in the students.

About The Department

The Discipline of Software Engineering was introduced in the year 2009. The department is currently running a B.Tech program in Software Engineering with an intake of 180 , an M.Tech program in Software Engineering with an intake of 25 and offers Ph.D. in the Discipline of Computer Science and Software Engineering. All the software engineering programs are well designed keeping in view the industry demands. The programs are designed to build the analytical and practical capabilities of students in the design and development of the software and lays emphasis on following well defined and systematic approach for meeting the growing demands and requirements of the software industry. The curriculum is regularly updated in accordance with the latest trends, research and best practices in the software industry and academia.
