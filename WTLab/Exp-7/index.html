Courses Offered

B.Tech

Delhi Technological University offers four year BTech programme across 15 specialisations. The admission process is conducted via Joint Admission Counselling (JAC) Delhi.

M.Tech

M.Tech is a professional two years postgraduate master degree programme. Delhi Technological University (DTU), Delhi offers admissions into M.Tech programmes based on GATE scores and in some cases, based on online test.

Ph.D

The University offers Ph.D. programme in wide range of areas in Engineering, Sciences and Humanities. The academic programme of Ph.D. degree is broad-based and involves a course credit requirement.

B.Des

DTU has decided to provide facilities for excellent design education and training to suit the needs of the society. Department of Design is envisioned to pursue excellence in design thinking, scholarship and practice.

About Delhi Technological University

Delhi Technological University (DTU), formerly known as the Delhi College of Engineering (DCE) is a state university in New Delhi, India. It was established in 1941 as Delhi Polytechnic. The institute has been under the Government of Delhi since 1963.

Campus

DTU has 164 acres of a lush gree, tech-savvy main campus, with approximately 1,56,000 square metres of built up area, 15 academic departments, research centres, and residences for students, faculty and staff.
